# Eglis Cut Club

## Brand Overview

Eglis Cut Club is an accessible, energetic and community-driven barbershop created by Eglis.

The business began when Eglis started cutting his friends’ hair during quarantine. What began as something fun became a genuine passion. He later completed professional barber training, earned his certification and developed approximately three years of experience serving both relaxed customers and highly detail-oriented clients.

The brand currently operates from two locations:

* Saranda — seasonal beachside caravan location
* Elbasan — year-round location

The Saranda location operates during June, July, August and September.

## Brand Positioning

Eglis Cut Club is a clean, affordable and social barbershop for people who want a quality haircut without the formality of a traditional salon.

The Saranda location offers a memorable experience inside a converted caravan. Customers can arrive directly from the beach, reserve a convenient time online, enjoy hip-hop music and conversation, and leave ready for the rest of their day or night.

## Tone of Voice

The brand communicates like a confident and funny local friend.

The language should be:

* Brief
* Direct
* Playful
* Friendly
* Informal
* Clear for international visitors

The brand should avoid luxury language, formal salon terminology and aggressive masculine clichés.

## Reservation System

Customers must choose:

1. Location
2. Service
3. Date
4. Available time
5. Name
6. Phone number

Customers do not need to create an account.

Payment is completed at the shop.

There is no online payment.

There is no email requirement.

There is no automatic SMS, WhatsApp or email confirmation.

After booking, the website must display a confirmation screen containing:

* Customer name
* Phone number
* Location
* Service
* Price
* Date
* Time
* Payment at shop notice
* Cancel reservation option
* Phone number for assistance

The confirmation page should encourage the customer to take a screenshot.

## Cancellation Rules

Customers can cancel at any time.

There is currently no cancellation deadline or penalty.

The cancellation process should be simple and should not require an account.

## Fully Booked Behaviour

When no appointments remain for a selected day, display:

“Fully booked for today.”

Then display:

* Other available dates
* Shop phone number
* A tap-to-call button

Suggested secondary message:

“Need help finding a time? Call Eglis.”

## Website Goal

The main goal of the website is to generate online reservations.

The reservation button should always be clearly visible, especially on mobile devices.

The website should be expressive and memorable without making booking difficult.

## Visual Identity

### Colors

Primary:

* Caravan cream
* Warm off-white
* Near-black
* Dark walnut brown
* Muted metallic grey

Accent:

* Faded red or burnt orange

Avoid bright tropical blues, neon colors, luxury gold and overly colorful beach branding.

### Typography

Use:

* A bold, distinctive sans-serif font for headlines
* A clean, highly readable sans-serif font for body text and booking information
* Large editorial-style headlines
* Strong spacing
* Minimal decorative elements

### Logo Direction

Main logo:

EGLIS
CUT CLUB

Secondary mark:

ECC

Avoid:

* Barber poles
* Scissors
* Beard silhouettes
* Crowns
* Shields
* Generic vintage barber logos

The logo should be primarily typographic and should work clearly on the caravan, clothing, signs, stickers and mobile screens.

## Photography

The website should prioritize authentic photography showing:

* The caravan exterior
* The interior barber chair
* Eglis working
* Customers arriving from the beach
* Friends talking or waiting
* Haircut transformations
* Clean tools and work surfaces
* The surrounding Saranda environment
* Day-to-night atmosphere

The website should not consist only of close-up fade photographs.

## Languages

* Italian

Suggested phrase:

Albanian, English, Italian—and somehow we’ll understand the rest.

## Expansion Vision

Eglis Cut Club should be designed as a scalable coastal brand.

Future location naming can follow this structure:

* Eglis Cut Club — Saranda
* Eglis Cut Club — Elbasan
* Eglis Cut Club — Ksamil
* Eglis Cut Club — Himara

The brand should remain connected to Eglis while being flexible enough to employ additional barbers and expand to more locations.
